Adapted from the Compass Rose spread from the Magician's Hand of Fate, which is another one of my ongoing projects. The Compass Rose is a five-card spread that is used to generate the basic outline of a oneshot or fetch quest. The description I have written is, "To find what you need and what you do not yet know you need, whether that be a person, a place, or a thing."

The concept is similar to what I have going on in Fetch Quest, but this generator is more detailed.
