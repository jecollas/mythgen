Generator Notes

Plot Type: Basic generator that draws from a list of plot types, genres, and scene tones.

Fetch Quest: Adapted from the Querent's Quest spread from the Magician's Hand of Fate (another one of my ongoing projects). The Querent's Quest is a seven-card spread that is used to generate the basic outline of a oneshot or fetch quest.

Adventure: Based on the Adventure Seed Generator by Johnn Four.

NPC Builder: Based on Reedsy's Fantasy Plot Generator.

Rumors: Based on the Rumor Maker by Myth's very own Andrew Warzocha.

Names: Based on a B/X D&D Name Generator that Eric sent me. Each of these syllables was collected from a proper name from the B/X Rulebooks and B/X modules, or was from the name of an early D&D author.

Plot Builder: An amalgamation of (and improvement on) Plot Type, Fetch Quest, Adventure, NPC Builder, and Rumors.

Monsters: NEWEST ADDITION! Still in progress, and still trying to figure out how this one will be used.
